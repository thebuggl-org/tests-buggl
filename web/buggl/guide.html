About the Local

Hi, I'm Kate Beckinsale

Editor of Lifestyle Magazine, Sydney local since 1999, likes fine wine, local cuisine, speak english and some french. Crossing Cultures and Bridging Divides in the Information Age. Strategic communications, marketing, pr, social media & tech from the Prairie to the World.

I love to share my knowledge about the city, give recommendations and info. I'm a traveler too and I know how important it is to have tips from locals... I'm also a yoga teacher and I love all outdoor activities. I love to cook and drink good wine, maybe while listening to some good jazz music!
