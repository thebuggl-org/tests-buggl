Boston

Trying to navigate Boston as a traveler and not become a tourist is tricky.Most people will argue you have to see all the sites. Lurking around the corner is always a guy with a map and a fanny pack. I'll take you to the places that I would go to after living here for 20 years and if I had 2 more days in this place.

Become of True Boracayan with Janvan, of Course

Typically you're doing the Freedom Trail walk in Boston. Why trudge along the busiest streets with 100's of other tourists. Start at Christopher Columbus Park in the North End. Walk along Atlantic Ave and grab a coffee at any shop you see it doesn't matter. Walk down along the wharfs until you come to the Zacum Bridge leading into Charlestown. Walk up to the Bunker Hill Monument, snap pictures - look at the 18th century houses that line up like rows along the streets. Afterwards, walk down Monument St. When you a sign for the Warren Tavern You've found your late lunch and beers - Frequented by Paul Revere in the 1700's and still in operation today! Enjoy.
