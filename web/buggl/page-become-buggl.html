Our Local Authors are...

Musicians, travel guidebook writers, award-winning photo journalists, professional surfers, top chefs, winemakers, leading food bloggers, newspaper editors, film makers, entrepreneurs, teachers, tour guides, and just regular guys and gals.

We expect...

  • A high level of passion and knowledge about where you live.

Why be a Buggl Author

The heartbeat of any city or region of the world is its people. These people are what make Buggl so special. We're creating a revolution in how travelers gather information on what to do, see, eat, drink and explore when they're out and about traveling or living. For far too long everyone has relied on travel agents, tour operators and outdated guidebooks to plan a trip. The time has come that local people with an inside scoop began contributing, earning income and disrupting the status quo of that experience. Being an author on our platform means being on the front lines of that change; we hope you join us!.

  • Make global friends.

    Connect and share knowledge with travelers from around the world thirsty for your advice and secrets. Seek others like you out on your next trip away...

  • Tell your story.

    Enjoy the excitement of telling people like you how to eat, drink, see and experience where you live. Give people the real story behind your city, country or region.

  • Earn Money.

    Make additional income or turn this love for your area into a real business. It's time you put your knowledge to use and helped travelers gather real travel advice.

  • Help your community.

    Tell travelers the right places to go and things to do. Bring awareness to lesser known events and locations otherwise forgotten from old travel guides and websites.

What every Buggl Author received in return:

  • Flexible Pricing

    Set the price of each of your travel guides based on what you think your knowledge is worth and adjust accordingly.

  • Online Transactions

    Buggl handles all payments to ensure you get paid, and on time. Our platform allows you to accept credit cards from anywhere in the world, securely.

  • Easy-to-use

    Our build a guide process allows you to easily create, edit and promote your travel guides at anytime. The Buggl dashboard gives you complete control over monitoring your payments and viewing customer data.

  • Unlimited Travel Guides

    Create just one guidebook or multiple. Share your experiences on everything you love and know. Our platform gives you the freedom to tell your story no matter how weird, funny or far out it may seem. There's always someone on the other side of world who is like you!
